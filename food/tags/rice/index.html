Risotto

For 2 portions: 1/4 onion 1/3 carrot 1 glass wine 8 handfuls rice 1 litre veg stock 1 knob butter Parmesan (lots) 1/ Heat oil in pan. Finely chop onion and carrot (no garlicl). Heat oil in pan and cook until soft. 2/ Add rice and cook until seethrough-ish. 3/ Add wine, cook until it’s all absorbed. 4/ Add stock 1 spoon at a tine, constantly stirring.
